Recent Project: Stratus CubeSat

Shown above, Michigan Technological Univeristy’s Aerospace Enterprises third satellite, named Stratus, will launch into Low Earth Orbit in mid 2018. Under contract with NASA, it’s onboard FLIR imager will help scientists understand the formation of clouds.

MTU’s Aerospace Enterprise is a student-run organization that designs, builds, and operates satellites after launch. Working with industry leaders like NASA, SpaceX, the US Air Force, and the Department of Defense, no other univsersity produces graduates with the same level hands-on experience than Michigan Tech.
